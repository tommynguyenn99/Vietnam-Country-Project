Discovering Vietnam: Top 5 Must-Do Experiences

Hang Son Doong

Son Doong is considered to be the largest cave in the world, based on volume and also the biggest cave in Vietnam, found by Ho Khanh a Phong Nha jungle man in 1990. In 2009, Hang Son Doong was officially surveyed and measured by the The British -Vietnam Cave Expedition Team led by Howard Limbert.The Expedition team then announced it as the world's largest natural cave with a volume of 38.5 million m3 -Oxalisadventure.com

Visit The Heart of Vietnam's Coffee Industry

Attention to all cofffe lovers, this is a must visit place! Vietnam is the world's second-largest coffee producer, which means that visiting the Buon Ma Thuot coffee plantations is a must on your trip. Discover Robusta coffee, grown in Vietnam’s central highlands. At the beginning of the 20th century, French settlers imported the first coffee plants for cultivation in the extremely fertile red soil around the city of Buon Ma Thuot. -Evaneos.com/

The Golden Hands Bridge in Da Nang

The Golden Bridge in Da Nang is a stunning 150 meter long pedestrian bridge that offers breathtaking views and a unique architectural design. It connects a cable car station with the gardens and has two giant stone-like hands supporting the structure, making it a must-visit tourist attraction. -Wikipedia

Ban Gioc Waterfall

Ban Gioc is the largest waterfall in Vietnam with a 300m span and a 30m drop, making this the widest waterfall in Vietnam. It straddles the border of one side being China and the other being Vietnam, being in a unique location this makes it an attractive tourist destination.-Wikitravel.org

Nighttime Adventure for the Party-Going Crowd

Today many cities like Hoi an, Hanoi, and Ho Chi Minh in Vietnam are offering the best nightlife in Vietnam with clubs, bars, and other places that are fit for the night owls. There are casinos and cruises where you can spend a leisurely evening and enjoy the best food. And the most exciting thing is that the place is quite affordable and will fit your budget.-myholidays.com
